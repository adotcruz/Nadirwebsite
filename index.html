The Student Marijuana Alliance for Research & Transparency

Mission Statement

The Student Marijuana Alliance for Research & Transparency (SMART) is a student-led network dedicated to learning openly, honestly, and transparently about cannabis and its underestimated role in our society. The SMART is a grassroots organization that creates affective change by addressing the public skepticism of marijuana with credible knowledge, real experiences, and real people. Founded in 2017, The SMART is currently based out of Brown University in Providence, RI.

Objective

The objective of The SMART is to provide students and those within the greater Brown community with the opportunity to learn judgement-free about the health, spiritual, and economical benefits of cannabis/hemp while exploring the existing sociological stigmas of current cannabis culture and how marijuana prohibition has destroyed communities of color.

Goals

The goal of The SMART is to provide university community members with informational workshops, community outreach events, and opportunities to engage in honest, transparent dialogue with experts within the cannabis/hemp industry. The ultimate goal of The SMART being to have Brown adopt interdisciplinary cannabis research & education, and to also create a pipeline for careers in the booming cannabis/hemp industry.
